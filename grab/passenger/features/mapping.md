### ⚙️ Technical Logic Summary
Grab implements its own mapping infrastructure (GrabMaps) optimized for Southeast Asia. It uses Karta for crowdsourced points-of-interest, ensuring map accuracy in regions where traditional mapping services may lack detail (e.g., narrow alleys, new developments).
